SETTING: Fredericksburg is a city of 10,000 residents an hour and a half by car from both Austin and San Antonio in the hill country, a part of the state known for its comparatively green, sloping landscape. Founded in the mid-1800s by Germans, the core of Fredericksburg is characterized by one- and two-story limestone structures, many on the National Register of Historic Places. For a small city, Fredericksburg has a good deal of boutique shopping, night life and bars, some of which tap into the many local vineyards. This house is on a 16-acre property 15 minutes from the city center in a rural area where, according to the listing agent, properties can run over 100 acres. An adjoining 90-acre lot is on the market for $765,000.
周边环境：弗雷德里克斯堡是一座拥有10000名居民的城市，从奥斯汀市(Austin)和圣安东尼奥市(San Antonio)开车途经丘陵地带，都是一个半小时即可到达。这里是德克萨斯州的一部分，以其相对葱茏的斜坡地貌而为人所知。弗雷德里克斯堡由德国人于19世纪中期建造，其中心地带以单层或双层的石灰石建筑为特色，其中许多房屋都登上了国家历史遗迹名录(National Register of Historic Places)。虽然这是一座小城市，但市内仍有许多精品服饰店、夜生活场所和酒吧。一些酒吧和当地的许多葡萄园都有联系。这栋房屋占地16英亩（约6.47公顷），位于乡间，距市中心15分钟。据该房源的经纪人说，该乡村区域的房产面积可达上百英亩（约40.47公顷）。附近就有一处90英亩（约36.42公顷）的地块，市场售价为76.5万美元（约合人民币474.1万元）。
INDOORS: The two-story limestone main house was built in the 1880s, and restored and expanded in the 1980s and ’90s. It is used as a bed-and-breakfast. Original features include longleaf pine floors, pine-paneled ceilings and 24-inch-thick stone walls. The great room, part of an addition, has a wood-burning fireplace and tile floors. Stainless-steel kitchen appliances were installed during renovations, as was a longleaf pine island. One bedroom is on the main level; the other, which is larger, is upstairs. It has a pitched pine-paneled ceiling and opens to a private balcony with views over the property.
室内环境：这栋用石灰石修葺的两层主屋，建于19世纪80年代，并于20世纪80年代到90年代经历过翻修和扩建。房屋目前被用作一处家庭旅馆。屋内的一些原始建筑特色，包括长叶松木地板、松木板条天花板和24英寸（约61厘米）厚的石壁。其中的大房间是扩建的一部分，带一座燃柴壁炉和铺有瓷砖的地板。翻修期间还加装了一些不锈钢厨房电器，以及一座长叶松木做的岛台。一间卧室位于主楼层；另外一间较大的卧室则在楼上。这间卧室有倾斜的松木板条天花板，并通往一个可以俯瞰整片属地的私人阳台。
Of the guesthouses, also part of the B & B, one is a two-story, two-bedroom house with a wood-burning fireplace in the den and a jetted tub in the bathroom. The other two are smaller single-story one-bedroom cottages.
客用房也是家庭旅馆的一部分。其中一栋是带两间卧室的两层房屋，其中的小隔间内有一座燃柴壁炉，浴室里有喷水浴缸。另外两栋，则是面积较小的单卧室单层小屋。
OUTDOOR SPACE: The property is shaded by old-growth oak trees and has a pool. The owner regularly sees deer and turkey.
户外空间：这处房产坐落于一片古老的橡树林中，还带有一个泳池。业主常能看见小鹿和火鸡。

HOW MUCH: $995,000
价格：99.5万美元（约合人民币616.6万元）
SIZE: 3,593 square feet
大小：3593平方英尺（约333.8平方米）

SETTING: Wildwood is a city of about 36,000 people 30 minutes by car from downtown St. Louis. This house is on a winding, forested road within a few miles from three state parks and preserves, including one along the Missouri River. The surrounding area is hilly, according to the listing agent, lots are zoned for three acres or more.
周边环境：怀尔伍德是一座人口大约3.6万的城市，从圣路易斯(St. Louis)市中心开车30分钟可达。这栋房屋位于一条蜿蜒的林荫大道旁，距此几英里的范围内，有三座国家公园和保护区，其中一座位于密苏里河(Missouri River)沿岸。据该房源的代理人说，此地周边是山丘地带，地块按3英亩（约1.21公顷）或大于3英亩的面积划分。
INDOORS: The two-story house, a reproduction of a 1750s saltbox, was completed in 1981. Period details include reclaimed wood floors, low ceilings with exposed beams, hand-planed wall paneling and handblown windows. Even the nails were restruck by hand to give the appearance of age.
室内环境：这栋仿造18世纪50年代的不对称双坡顶房屋而建的两层建筑，竣工于1981年。其中一些有时代特色的细节，包括回收再利用的木地板、有裸露屋梁的低矮天花板、手工刨制的壁板，以及人工吹制的窗玻璃。甚至所用钉子都是手工重铸的，以便呈现出岁月的痕迹。
In addition to formal living and dining rooms, there’s a family room with a pitched ceiling. All three rooms have brick, wood-burning fireplaces. There’s also a library, and a sitting room with doors opening to a stone terrace, one of three on the property. Kitchen appliances and surfaces include a Jenn-Air range, a Sub-Zero refrigerator, copper counters and double ceramic sinks.
屋内除了有正式的客厅与餐厅，还有一间带斜顶天花板的家庭娱乐室。这三个房间都有砖砌的燃柴壁炉。另外还有一间书房和一间起居室，房门通往一处石砌的平台，这是该房产三处平台中的一处。厨房电器和操作台包括一个尊爵牌(Jenn-Air)炉灶、一台Sub-Zero的冰箱、铜质台面和陶瓷双格水槽。
There are two bedrooms on the first floor, including the master. It has a brick fireplace and two built-in window seats. The other two bedrooms are upstairs. Both have fireplaces with wood-panel surrounds. The walkout lower level has a bedroom and a bathroom with a jetted tub and a sauna. Also on the property is a three-car garage with a studio with a brick floor.
一楼有两间卧室，包括一间主卧。主卧中有一座砖砌的壁炉和两处窗边的嵌壁式座椅。另外两间卧室在楼上，都带有四周包木板条的壁炉。带出口的地下室中有一间卧室和一间带喷水浴缸与桑拿房的浴室。这处房产还有一间带三个车位的车库，内有一处地面铺砖的工作间。
OUTDOOR SPACE: The property is a little over nine acres. The area in front of the house was landscaped so as to attract wildlife, with pathways weaving through tall grass. Behind the house, the property slopes into a valley.
户外空间：这处房产占地面积9英亩（约3.64公顷）多一点。房屋正前方做了园林美化，以吸引野生动物，几条小径在高高的草丛中起伏，屋后的土地则向山谷方向倾斜而去。
