Donald J. Trump on Wednesday released a financial disclosure form that all presidential candidates are required to file with the Federal Election Commission. The form includes estimates of his assets, income and debt since the beginning of 2015, but it provides much less specificity than his tax returns would, should he release them.
周三，唐纳德·J·特朗普(Donald J. Trump)公布了一份财务报表。所有的总统候选人都必须向联邦选举委员会(Federal Election Commission)提交这样的报表。内容包括从2015年初起的资产、收入和债务估算额。不过如果他能公布自己的纳税申报表的话，会比这份报表具体得多。

资产：至少15亿美元

The bulk of Mr. Trump’s income over the filing period came from his golf courses and resorts and from condominium sales and rents from his real estate developments. The disclosure he released in 2015, which covered a similar period of time beginning in January 2014, showed income totaling at least $380 million.
