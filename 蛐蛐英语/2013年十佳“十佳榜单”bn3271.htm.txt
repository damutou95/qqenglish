It’s that joyous time of year again: end-of-year list season! Two years ago I sang the praises of listmaking in the magazine, discussing the fun of “creating, in 10 increments, the person you want to be.” As 2013 comes to a close, it’s a wonderful chance to look back on a year of listmaking. Which critics are working at the peak of the Top 10 form? Who is reinvigorating the tropes of the best list, finding new ways to tell old stories? Let’s take a look at the Top 10 Top 10 Lists of 2013.
又是一年中的欢乐时刻了：年末榜单季！两年前，我曾在《纽约时报杂志》上为列榜单唱过赞歌，讨论过“用十种元素创造你希望成为的人”这类东西有多好玩。哪些评论家在“前十名排行榜”的领域居于顶尖地位？谁能让“最佳榜单”重新焕发出光彩，用新的方法讲述老故事？来看看2013年十佳榜单中的十佳榜单吧。
10. Rob Sheffield’s “10 Best Cassettes of 2013.” A wonderfully eclectic list of music you almost certainly can’t listen to, released on “the scrappy little format that refuses to die,” written up lovingly by Sheffield, the estimable Rolling Stone columnist. I love this list not only because Sheffield clearly adores these 10 cassettes, but also because the entire enterprise functions as a next-level mike-dropping: Oh, I’m making a Top 10 list of albums I bought on iTunes or whatever? Rob Sheffield accumulated so many cassette tapes in 2013 that he has to winnow those down.
10. 罗布·谢菲尔德(Rob Sheffield)的“2013年十佳卡带”。这是一份极为客观中立的音乐榜单，你几乎肯定不会去听。谢菲尔德，这位可敬的《滚石》(Rolling Stone)专栏作家满含感情地写道，这些卡带“拒绝死亡，以混乱的小众形式发行”。我喜欢这份榜单，不仅仅是因为谢菲尔德喜欢这十张卡带，也是因为整个行业都在进一步放弃：啊，我列了一份十佳专辑榜单，都是我从iTunes之类地方买来的？罗布·谢菲尔德在2013年收集了那么多卡带，所以他必须选出这个十佳榜单。
9. Emily Nussbaum’s “I Really Do Hate Top 10 Lists.” The New Yorker’s excellent television critic writes her annual end-of-the-year roundup, including her annual end-of-the-year jeremiad against listmaking. (Here’s 2012’s version, and 2011’s.) Nussbaum claims her 33 favorite shows “are not in any order, because that would be insane,” but I prefer to read the list as a secret message from Emily Nussbaum’s subconscious and then demand to know how the heck she could rank “The Middle” over “Breaking Bad.” What on earth, Emily Nussbaum?!
9. 艾米莉·纳斯本(Emily Nussbaum)的“我真的很讨厌列十大榜单”。这位《纽约客》(New Yorker)出色的电视评论家写了她的年底例行总结，其中也写到她每年年底例行列十佳榜单时的伤心史（这里是她2012年和2011年的版本）。纳斯本说，她最喜欢的33个电视节目“次序不分先后，因为排序让人发疯”，但我更愿意把这份榜单看做她潜意识的秘密流露，想知道她怎么能把《中产家庭》(The Middle)列在《绝命毒师》(Breaking Bad)前面。到底怎么回事呀，艾米莉·纳斯本？！
8. David Rees’s “Top 10 Trends of 2013.” No one limns trends like David Rees. NO ONE.
8. 大卫·里斯(David Rees)的“2013年十大潮流”。没有人能像大卫·里斯那样勾勒出时尚的大潮。没有人。
7. Jeffrey Kluger’s “Top 10 New Species of 2013.” This list, from Time magazine, had a real shot at No. 1 on my list. Each new species, from the carnivorous olinguito to Luchihormetica luckae, the glow-in-the-dark cockroach, was more interesting than the last! Then Kluger had to screw it up by including two extinct species at Nos. 1 and 2 — a clear violation of the code of listmaking. I don’t care how cool your dinosaur is, it’s not one of the top 10 new species of 2013.
7. 杰弗里·克拉格(Jeffrey Kluger)的“2013年十大新物种”。这个榜单来自《时代》(Time)杂志，本来真的很有可能冲击我这份榜单的头名位置。这些新物种包括食肉的小型犬浣熊，乃至“Luchihormetica luckae”，一种在黑暗中发光的蟑螂，真是一个比一个有意思！但是后来克拉格就搞砸了，他把两种已经灭绝的物种分别列为第一名和第二名，这破坏了列榜单的规矩。我不在乎你的恐龙有多酷，它又不是2013年的十大新物种啊。
6. Newepicfail1’s “10 Greatest Farts in Live TV History.” I don’t know anything about YouTube user newepicfail1, nor about this video, which has a mere 5,000 views at this writing. I don’t know why the video is so shoddily edited or where these clips came from. I certainly don’t know if any of these examples of breaking bad on television are true or if they are all hoaxes. I do know that we are entering a brave new world of video listmaking, and newepicfail1 is leading the charge.
6. Newepicfail1的“现场电视节目史上十大蠢货”。我不认识这位网名newepicfail1的YouTube用户，也不了解这个视频的情况，我写这篇文章的时候，它只被观看过5000次。我不知道这个视频为什么剪辑得这么差劲，也不知道素材都从哪儿来。我不知道视频里那些上了电视的傻瓜们是不是真的，又或者仅仅是恶作剧而已。我不知道我们是不是进了一个视频榜单的美丽新世界，这个世界里由newepicfail1说了算。
5. Chris Sims’s “10 Worst Holiday Gifts for Comic Book Readers.” A very funny list, from Comics Alliance, that mixes trenchant comics criticism (DC’s “New 52 Omnibus” really is stupid) with excellent, terrible Amazon finds (a huge pillow printed with Tom Hiddleston’s face).
5. 克里斯·西姆斯(Chris Sims)的“送给漫画读者的十大最差节日礼物”。非常有趣的榜单，来自“漫画同盟”(Comics Alliance)，其中既有犀利的漫画评论（DC的《新52选集》[New 52 Omnibus]真的很蠢），也有从亚马逊网站精心找到的可怕玩意儿（比如一个大枕头，上面印着汤姆·希德勒斯顿[Tom Hiddleston]的脸）。
4. Spin’s “50 Best Albums of 2013.” Of course, it’s often no longer enough to create a Top 10 — and so we see Top 15s, Top 25s, and even (from magazines and websites as opposed to individual critics, generally) Top 50s and Top 100s. But Spin’s list was nonetheless exemplary, appearing early in the game (Dec. 2) and reintroducing several albums into the conversation that might otherwise have been lost. (How many music writers went back and listened to Caitlin Rose’s excellent album “The Stand-In” when they saw it at No. 25?) The only problem with publishing your list early is you miss late-to-the-game releases — so, no “Beyoncé.”
4. 《Spin》杂志的“2013年50张最佳专辑”。当然，现在“十佳专辑”往往已经不够用了，我们经常见到“15张最佳专辑”，“25张最佳专辑”，甚至（往往是来自杂志和网站，而不是评论家个人）50张最佳专辑和100张最佳专辑。但是不管怎么说，《Spin》的榜单都堪称典范，它在业界出台极早（12月2日就出来了），把几张很可能被人遗忘的专辑重新引入讨论中（比如榜单中的第25名，柴特林·罗斯[Caitlin Rose]精彩的《替身》[The Stand-In]，有多少乐评人已经把它忘了，得折回去再听听？）。过早发布榜单带来的唯一的问题是，你可能会遗漏一年中较晚发行的专辑——所以，《碧昂斯》(Beyoncé)没有入选。
3. Claudia La Rocco on Performance in 2013. Is it kosher for me to list this Artforum list, given that I didn’t experience any of the amazing-sounding performance art, theater and dance therein? I think so. The list, after all, kicks off with a performance La Rocco didn’t see, and its heart just gets bigger from there.
3. 克劳迪娅·拉·罗科(Claudia La Rocco)的“2013年现场演出”榜单。其实我从来没有体验过这些听上去很棒的表演艺术，不论是舞台剧还是舞蹈，所以我把这份Artforum的榜单列进来真的合适吗？不管怎么说，这份榜单以一个拉·罗科自己根本没看过的演出开始，之后开始变得愈发开阔。
2. The Playlist Staff’s “Top 10 Shots of 2013.” Well-written, surprising, revelatory, will change the way you look back at 2013’s movies. As is traditional, No. 2 should actually be No. 1.
2. “播放列表”(The Playlist)工作人员列出的“2013年十佳镜头”。文笔精彩，令人惊讶，有启发性，能改变你看待2013年电影的方式。和往年一样，第二名其实比第一名好。
1. Wesley Morris’s “Top 10 Best (Favorite) Movies of 2013.” One of the best film critics in the country produces the best Top 10 list of the year at Grantland – complete with classic No. 9 (“This Is the End”), a fantastic selection of foreign and art films you most likely haven’t yet seen, and a 1-2 punch at the top that epitomizes why lists are worth making. “The Wolf of Wall Street” and “12 Years a Slave” are about as apple-and-orangey as movies get, and Wesley Morris has no problem ranking them — just like I have no problem arguing that he’s wrong.
1. 韦斯利·莫里斯(Wesley Morris)的“2013年（我最喜欢的）十佳电影”。这是本国最优秀的影评人之一在Grantland评出的一份最优秀的十佳榜单。其中第九名《世界末日》(This Is the End)尤为经典，这是一个奇异的榜单，既有外国电影，也有艺术电影，你很可能根本没看过。此外其中有一两个选择犹如当头一棒，可以让人明白，为什么列榜单这种事值得一做。《华尔街之狼》(The Wolf of Wall Street)和《为奴12年》(12 Years a Slave)的优劣见仁见智，韦斯利·莫里斯在为它们排名次时毫不犹豫——就像我会毫不犹豫地指出：他是错的。
