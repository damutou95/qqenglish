SETTING: This house is in Capitol Hill, a neighborhood of 19th-century rowhouses in the heart of the city. The Capitol, the Supreme Court building, the Library of Congress and offices for both the Senate and House are all about a mile away, as are the city’s major museums and monuments, including the Smithsonian, the Hirshhorn and the National Mall. Pennsylvania Avenue, the area’s shopping-and-dining corridor, is about five blocks away. So is the Eastern Market, a farmer’s market in operation since the late 1800s. The house is two and a half blocks from Lincoln Park, site of the original Lincoln monument.
周边环境：这栋房屋位于国会山(Capitol Hill)上，华盛顿中心地带的一个19世纪排屋住宅区内。方圆一英里(约1.6公里)处有国会大厦、最高法院大楼、国会图书馆和参众两院的办公楼，还有市内主要的几座博物馆和纪念馆，包括史密森尼博物馆(Smithsonian)、希尔修博物馆(Hirshhorn)及国家广场(National Mall)。当地的购物餐饮一条街宾夕法尼亚大道(Pennsylvania Avenue)与此处房产大约相隔五条街区。那里还有一家自19世纪晚期开始营业的农贸市场——城东市场(Eastern Market)。两条半街区之外就是林肯公园(Lincoln Park)，也即林肯纪念馆原来的所在地。
INDOORS: The two-story cedar-sided house was built shortly before the Civil War and last renovated in the late 1990s. Through the front door is a living room with a gas stove, followed by a galley kitchen with Jenn-Air appliances and a breakfast bar. Floors are light-colored hardwood and kitchen cabinets are white with glass doors. Sliding glass doors in the kitchen open to a bedroom used as a solarium, with pitched cedar-beam ceilings, glass-brick walls, a copper roof and a large skylight. The second bedroom is upstairs. A niche in the hallway has a shelf made from one of the original stair treads, worn down by longtime use.
室内环境：这栋两层房屋建于内战前不久，屋侧种有雪松，最后一次翻修是在二十世纪九十年代晚期。从前门进去，是一间带燃气壁炉的客厅，紧相连是一间船舱式的简约厨房(galley kitchen)，内有几样尊爵牌(Jenn-Air)电器和一个早餐吧台。地板是浅色实木的，橱柜为白色，带玻璃门。厨房的一扇玻璃推拉门通往一间被用作日光房的卧室，这个房间有倾斜的雪松横梁天花板、几面玻璃砖墙、铜质的屋顶及一扇大天窗。第二间卧室位于楼上。走廊中的一处壁龛内，装有以一截原来的楼梯踏板做成的格架，木质经长期使用已有磨损。
OUTDOOR SPACE: The solarium opens to a Virginia bluestone patio and gardens. There’s also a bike shed.
户外空间：那间日光房通往一座弗吉尼亚式(Virginia)的青石平台和一片花园。另有一处自行车棚。

SETTING: Cerrillos is a rural community of about 300 people, 25 miles from Santa Fe. This house is on 30 acres of high desert. It has horse facilities, a chicken coop, a geodesic-domed greenhouse, a garage and an old railroad car used for storage. Power comes from a solar photovoltaic system, and water from a private well. According to the listing agent, the entire subdivision is off-grid and environmentally sustainable. Technically in Cerrillos, this house is closer to Madrid, a mining village turned artists’ colony.
周边环境：塞里约斯是一个大约有300人的乡村社区，距圣达菲市(Santa Fe)25英里（约40公里）。这栋房屋位于一片面积30英亩（约12公顷）的高地荒原上，带有几处马厩设施、一间鸡舍、一间有格状穹顶的温室、一间车库和一截用作储藏室的旧铁路车厢。这里的电能来自一套太阳能光伏系统，而水源则来自一口私家水井。据该房产的代理人说，这里整片土地都处于供电网络之外，但环境上能自给自足。这栋房产理论上归属于塞里约斯，但其实距马德里(Madrid)更近。这个马德里，是由之前的采矿小山村发展成的一个艺术家聚居地。
INDOORS: The two-story adobe has rounded plaster walls, brick and wood floors, rough-cut vigas, ornamental tile and exposed brick walls throughout. Situated for passive solar heating, many of the rooms also have expansive views of surrounding mountain ranges.
室内环境：这栋两层楼的土坯房屋，有几面环形的石灰墙壁、铺着砖块地板和木地板、架着切割粗糙的屋梁、贴着饰面瓷砖，还有完全暴露的砖块。房屋所在的位置不仅适合采用被动式太阳能供暖，而且从许多房间内可以看见周围大片的山景。
The living room is sunken, with skylights, a wood stove and a long rounded wall with floor-to-ceiling windows. At one end of the living room is a rounded turret-like study with exposed brick walls. The kitchen and dining room have partial walls and are open to the living room. There is ground-floor bedroom suite with a private office and a bathroom with a claw-foot tub. Kitchen appliances were updated within the last five years. Upstairs, there’s a bedroom suite with 360-degree views of the Ortiz, Sangre de Cristo and Jemez Mountains. The main house has two interior patios. The one-bedroom log guesthouse has a sauna.
客厅是下沉式的，带有几扇天窗、一个燃木壁炉和一面装有落地窗的环形墙壁。这间客厅的一端，是一间圆形的塔楼状书房，带有裸露的砖墙。厨房和餐厅部分以墙面隔开，入口处朝向这间客厅。一个位于底层的卧室套间，带有一间私人办公室和一间有兽脚浴缸的浴室。厨房电器都是近五年内新添置的。楼上那个卧室套间，可以看见奥尔蒂斯(Ortiz)、基督圣血(Sangre de Cristo)和赫梅斯(Jemez)几大山脉的360度全景。主屋带有两处室内平台。而那栋单卧室的原木客用房内还有一间桑拿室。
