Carrie White has been remarkably resilient. Getting pelted with tampons for decades will do that to a girl.
嘉莉·怀特(Carrie White)可谓长盛不衰。几十年来被弄得像浸透了血的卫生棉，对一个女孩来说可真不容易。
Since the high school misfit with emerging telekinetic powers and a religious-fanatic mother first appeared in Stephen King’s 1974 novel, “Carrie,” her blood-streaked face and prom dress have been among the most recognizable images in horror. Brian De Palma’s 1976 movie version, starring the beautifully plain-faced Sissy Spacek, helped propel Carrie into pop culture consciousness with a scene that still scares the tiara off any aspiring prom queen. Shot in masochistic slow-motion, the sequence captures in grisly detail what happens when bullies rig up a bucket that douses her with pig’s blood onstage in front of her classmates. Sent into an uncontrollable rage, Carrie uses her powers to turn the gymnasium into an inferno and the scene into a horror-movie touchstone.
斯蒂芬·金(Stephen King)1974年的小说《魔女嘉莉》(Carrie)塑造了这样一个女主人公，她不适应中学生活，拥有超能力，同时又有一个宗教狂母亲；从那以后，她那流血的面孔和毕业礼服就成了最恐怖的形象之一。布莱恩·德·帕尔玛(Brian De Palma)1976年的电影由有着动人扁平面孔的茜茜·斯派塞克(Sissy Spacek)主演，令嘉莉的形象打入流行文化，片中的情景到现在仍然能令野心勃勃的毕业舞会皇后吓得头饰也掉下来。片中欺负嘉莉的人用水桶装上猪血，在舞台上当着同学们的面泼了她一身，一系列受虐狂式的慢镜头展示了可怕的细节。嘉莉陷入无法控制的狂怒，使用自己的力量让体育馆变成了地狱，这一幕堪称恐怖电影的里程碑。
That hair-raising mix of gore and revenge has inspired composers to write stage musicals, fashion designers to create splatter-patterned costumes, knitters to sell Carrie-theme teddy bears on Etsy.com — and now Hollywood to produce a remake (due Friday). The slide show features some of the many versions (almost always titled “Carrie”) of the picked-on heroine’s signature look.
