It has been 40 years since the French furniture company Roche Bobois planted a flag in the United States, but only recently did it invite an American to design for its collection. 
法国家具公司罗奇堡(Roche Bobois)将业务范围扩大到美国已有四十余年，但是直到最近，这家法国公司才首次邀请一位美国人为它做设计。
On Thursday, the company is introducing two chairs by Stephen Burks, a New Yorker, that riff on consumer tastes in different parts of the world. Called Traveler, the chairs come in a European version with a canopied tubular metal structure and leather upholstery ($11,800 with canopy) and an American version in wood and fabric ($11,800). 
这周四（指6月5日——译注），该公司发布了两款由纽约人史蒂芬·伯克斯(Stephen Burks)设计的椅子，可以满足世界不同地区客户的品位。一款叫作“旅行者”(Traveler)，这款椅子的欧洲款是管状金属结构的，饰面材料为皮质的（带椅罩款的售价为11800美元[约合人民币73207元]）；而美国款则是木头和织物材质的（售价11800美元[约合人民币73207元]）。
Mr. Burks said the first chair expresses a “micro-architectural moment” and celebrates craft, whereas the second signals deep, satisfying comfort: “It’s like a giant pillow,” he said. But don’t take these ideas too literally, he added. “There is so much diversity within cultures, it would be a gross exaggeration to say, ‘I’ve summed up Europe in a chair, or summed up America.’ ” 
伯克斯说，第一款椅子表达了一种“微建筑时刻”，以及对手工艺的赞颂；而第二种，人往上一坐就深深陷了进去，非常舒服，“它就像一个巨大的枕头。”但是，千万别把这些想法太当真，他补充到。“文化是如此多元，要是说我能在一把椅子里总结出欧洲或美国，未免就过于夸张了。
