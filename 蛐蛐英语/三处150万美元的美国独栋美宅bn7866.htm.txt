SETTING: This 1953 house is in Seward Park, a neighborhood on Lake Washington in southeast Seattle. The neighborhood is named for a 300-acre park two blocks from this house, much of it covered in old-growth forest containing Douglas fir, hemlock, madrona and Alaskan cedar. Walking and biking trails throughout the park connect to a network of trails along the edge of the lake. Mostly residential, Seward Park has a handful of coffee shops and stores, while Columbia City, one neighborhood over, is an eclectic and low-key hub for shopping and dining.
周边环境：这栋建于1953年的房屋，坐落在西雅图市东南部的华盛顿湖(Lake Washington)岸边，在一个名为西沃德公园(Seward Park)的社区内。该社区得名于一座面积300英亩（约合121公顷）的公园，那里距离这栋房屋仅有两条街区。该公园的大部分面积都覆盖着原始森林，其中生长着花旗松、铁杉、野草莓树和阿拉斯加雪松。几条穿过公园、可供散步和骑行的小径，与湖边纵横交错的蜿蜒小道相连。虽然西沃德公园主体为住宅区，但这里还是有数十家咖啡馆和商店，而且，与其相隔一个社区的哥伦比亚城(Columbia City)是个包罗万象而又含蓄低调的购物与餐饮中心。
INDOORS: The two-story house was designed by Paul Hayden Kirk, a Seattle architect whose work included residences, churches, libraries and medical clinics, most in the International style. Kirk won several awards from the American Institute of Architects, and served as the Seattle chapter’s president.
室内环境：这栋两层的房屋由西雅图建筑师保罗·海登·柯克(Paul Hayden Kirk)设计，他的建筑作品包括住宅、教堂、图书馆和医院，大多数都是国际风格。柯克还获得过美国建筑师学会(American Institute of Architects)颁发的几个奖项，并担任了西雅图分会的主席。
The exterior of the house is cedar, with some accents of jagged basalt, a material that also makes up several interior walls. Living spaces are structured around a large central atrium that reaches through and above the roofline of the second story, where clerestory windows provide extra light. Floors are black slate throughout, windows are mostly floor-to-ceiling glass, and several of the rooms are partitioned by Japanese-style screens made of horsehair and resin, features that help provide a clean, transparent look. Interior paneling is sen, a light-colored wood similar in appearance to ash.
这栋房屋外面种植着雪松，还衬有一些嶙峋的玄武岩。这种材料也用于屋内的几面墙壁。房屋中的生活空间分布在宽敞的中庭区周围，该区域直通二层的屋顶，那里有几扇天窗用来加强采光。屋内地面全部铺着黑色石板，窗户大多是落地玻璃窗，还有几个房间以日式屏风隔开。这几扇屏风是用马鬃与松脂制成的，看上去干净透明。室内的墙面嵌板用的是水曲柳，这种浅色的木板看上去很像白蜡木。
The house is on a slope, with common areas and the master bedroom suite on the entry level, and the second bedroom suite on the lower level. Updates include stainless-steel kitchen appliances installed within the past 20 years, as were recessed ceiling lighting and a Sonos sound system.
这栋房屋坐落在一片山坡上，屋里的公共区域和主卧套间都在入口那层，次卧套间则在楼下那层。新换过的物品包括近20年内安装的一些不锈钢厨房电器，还有隐蔽式的天花板照明灯和Sonos音响系统。
The master bedroom is separated from the common spaces by a sitting area with a wood-burning fireplace set into a basalt wall. The bedroom has a balcony that overlooks the gardens. The updated bathrooms have heated floors. The lower-level bedroom has an original chandelier made from obsidian-colored ceramic pendants. It opens to a patio.
主卧与公共区域之间隔着一处休息区，一面玄武岩墙壁上有一座燃木壁炉。这间卧室中有一座可以俯瞰花园的阳台。翻修过的浴室内装有地热。楼下那间卧室中有一盏由黑曜石色陶瓷吊坠组成的原装枝形吊灯。这间卧室通往一处平台。
OUTDOOR SPACE: The house is on just over a third of an acre, with a small bridge leading from the interior atrium to gardens and a man-made creek, below which is a patio. Basalt benches echo the interior basalt walls. The property was designed by William G. Teufel, who landscaped the World’s Fair in Seattle in 1962.
户外空间：这栋房屋占地仅三分之一英亩（约1348平方米），中庭有一座小桥通往花园，还有一条人工开凿的小溪，下方有一处平台。几条玄武岩的长椅与屋内的玄武岩墙壁遥相呼应。这处房产的景观是由威廉·G·托伊费尔(William G. Teufel)设计的，该设计师还为1962年的西雅图世界博览会做过景观设计。
TAXES: $8,491 a year
税收：每年8491美元（约合人民币52244元）。

INDOORS: The single-story house was built in 1988 and updated last year. Construction is concrete, with a blue glazed-tile roof. Common areas take advantage of ocean views, with intersecting walls of sliding glass doors in the great room that open to a wraparound deck facing the water. Floors are white tile throughout. In the kitchen are stainless-steel appliances and marble surfaces, installed during renovations last year. Bedrooms are in a separate wing, joined to common areas by a sitting area with pitched wood-beam ceilings. All three bedrooms have sliding-glass doors opening to a second wraparound deck. The en-suite master bathroom has a soaking tub.
室内环境：这栋单层房屋建于1988年，去年曾经翻修过。房屋采用了混凝土建筑结构，带有蓝色琉璃瓦屋顶。屋内的公共区域充分利用了海景优势，最大的房间里有几面交叉的玻璃推拉门，门外是面对水域的全景平台。屋内地面铺的全部是白色瓷砖。厨房里有一些不锈钢电器和大理石台面，这些都是去年翻修期间新装的。几间卧室都位于房屋的另一侧，通过一处有倾斜木梁天花板的休息区，与公共区域相连。全部三间卧室都带有玻璃推拉门，可通往另一处全景平台。主卧套间里有一座可以泡澡的浴缸。
OUTDOOR SPACE: The house is on about an acre, lush with mature banyan, coconut and banana trees. Parking is in a covered spot under the house. There’s space to pull a pontoon boat in and out of the water.
户外空间：这栋房屋的占地面积约为1英亩（约4047平方米），周围种着很多已长大的榕树、椰子树和香蕉树。房屋下有一处带顶棚的地方用来停车，也有足够空间将一条平底船拖进或拖出水域。

It sits on a knoll overlooking Nantucket Sound, with a path leading from its deck to the sand.
周边环境：丹尼斯港是丹尼斯镇区域内的一座海滨小村，位于科德角(Cape Cod)的南岸。这座村庄里有许多建于20世纪40和50年代的别墅。据该房源的经纪人说，这里是个重视家庭且团结和睦的地方；这栋房屋35年来从未在房地产市场上出现过。它坐落在一座小山丘上，俯瞰着南塔基湾(Nantucket Sound)。还有一条小径，由别墅的平台通往沙滩。
