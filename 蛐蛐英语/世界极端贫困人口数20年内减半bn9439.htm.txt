While many Americans typically focus on the ups and downs of the national economy, something remarkable has been occurring among the world’s poorest nations. In just over two decades, the number of people living in extreme poverty has been almost halved.
许多美国人往往只关注国内经济的起伏，但世界上最贫困的那些国家正发生着一件不同寻常的事情。在短短二十多年的时间里，极端贫困人口的数量几乎减半。
The World Bank defines extreme poverty as a living standard supported by less than $1.25 a day, or $38 a month, in 2005 dollars. This is a very low threshold, and people with considerably more money than that are living very difficult lives. Still, using this measure, there is some excellent news: From 1990 to 2011, nearly a billion people around the world rose above abject poverty.
世界银行(World Bank)对极端贫困的定义是，按照2005年的美元币值计算，一天的生活费用低于1.25美元（约合7.7元人民币），也就是说一个月的生活费用低于38美元。这是一个极低的门槛，远比这个标准有钱的人的生活都非常困难。然而，按照这个标准，我们看到了一些喜人的消息：从1990年到2011年，全球近10亿人摆脱了极端贫困。
That’s not to say extreme poverty has ended. According to the World Bank’s analysis, there are still roughly one billion people whose total income is below that threshold — a level of material deprivation that many in the developed world can scarcely imagine.
这并不是说极端贫困结束了。根据世行的分析，依然大致有10亿人的总收入低于上述标准。发达世界的许多人几乎想象不到这种物质缺乏的程度。
Defining extreme poverty across different countries and cultures presents many problems. The World Bank’s figure is widely but not universally accepted. Other organizations, such as the Oxford Poverty and Human Development Initiative, use a calculation method that puts the number of people living in abject poverty at 1.6 billion. Still, there is agreement that extreme poverty has been on the decline since the mid-1990s and that the decline has accelerated since 2000.
不同国家和文化对极端贫困的定义引发了许多问题。世行的数据得到了广泛但并非一致的认可。牛津贫困与人类发展项目(Oxford Poverty and Human Development Initiative)等其他机构所用的计算方式，将处于极端贫困的人口数量定在16亿。然而，各方一致认为，自上世纪90年代中期以来，极端贫困的问题有所缓解，且自2000年以来，这种缓解在加速。
What’s behind the shift? Rising incomes in India and China are a major factor. Together, those two countries lifted 232 million people out of extreme poverty from 2008 to 2011 alone, according to the World Bank analysis. India, however, also faces the biggest challenge: It accounts for 30 percent of all people still living in extreme poverty.
是什么在推动这些变化？印度和中国的收入日渐增加是一个重要因素。根据世界银行的分析，仅从2008年到2011年，这两个国家就有2.32亿人脱离了极端贫困。然而，印度依然面临着最严峻的挑战：极端贫困人口中，印度占了30%。
