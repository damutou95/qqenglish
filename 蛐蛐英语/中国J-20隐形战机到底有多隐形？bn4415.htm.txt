When China unveiled its J-20 stealth fighter in 2011, analysts noted the aircraft’s potential to shift the regional balance of power further in the favor of the People’s Liberation Army Air Force. And the country is clearly committed to investing in military hardware. On Wednesday, China announced it was increasing its defense spending in 2014 to 808.23 billion renminbi, or about $132 billion, a rise of 12.2 percent.
中国在2011年首次展示了自己的歼-20隐形战斗机后，分析人士曾指出，该战斗机可能会将亚太地区的力量平衡进一步向人民解放军空军倾斜。中国显然仍致力于军事硬件的投资。周三，中国宣布其2014年军事预算增至8082.3亿元人民币，比上年增加了12.2%。
But many analysts have suggested that the J-20, ostensibly the showpiece of the air force’s modernization, had the “look” of being stealthy without actually incorporating many of the details that make aircraft difficult to detect on radar, as the defense journalist David Axe noted in The Diplomat in 2011.
然而，不少分析人士已指出，象征着人民解放军空军现代化的歼-20，有隐形战斗机的“样子”，但实际上不具有那些让飞机难以被雷达侦测到的细节，正如国防记者戴维·阿克斯(David Axe)2011年在《外交官》(The Diplomat)杂志上所写的。
For one thing, Mr. Axe wrote, “several rear-aspect photos seem to show traditional, fixed, round engine nozzles. The F-22, B-2 stealth bomber and now-retired F-117 stealth fighter-bomber all have carefully shaped, angular nozzles meant to scatter radar waves. In the F-22, these nozzles can move, ‘vectoring’ the engine thrust to boost maneuverability. The T-50 can pull the same vectoring trick with its round nozzles. 
阿克斯写道，首先“从一些对飞机后方拍摄的照片来看，(歼-20)看来具有传统、固定的圆型发动机喷口。而F-22、B-2隐形轰炸机，以及现已退役的F-117隐形战斗机，都具有仔细设计的有棱角的喷口，为的是散射雷达波。而且F-22的喷口还可调整方向，利用引擎推力‘矢量’增强可操控性。T-50的圆型喷口也具有同样的‘矢量’操控性。”
“The apparent absence of stealthy nozzles and thrust-vectoring places a hard limit on the J-20’s ability to evade radar detection from behind.”
“歼-20看来缺少隐型喷口和矢量推进能力，这是歼-20避开后方雷达侦测能力上的硬伤。”
But the three J-20’s seen so far are prototypes. Their manufacturer, Chengdu Aerospace Corporation, appears to be learning as it tests, based on photos circulating this week.
不过，这三架人们已经看到的歼-20都是原型机。从本周传出来的照片来看，它们的制造厂家、成都飞机工业公司似乎正在从飞行测试中学到东西。
The first prototype, serial number 2001, shows significant differences from the latest prototype, serial number 2011, in the position and serration of the landing gear doors, the shapes of the nose and engine intakes (just to the rear of the cockpit) and the shape of the vertical stabilizers, or tail fins.
序号为2001的第一架原型机，与序号为2011的最新原型机相比，有很大的区别，表现在起落架舱门的位置和锯齿、机头和引擎进气道（就在驾驶舱后面），以及垂直稳定翼或称尾翼的形状等方面。
