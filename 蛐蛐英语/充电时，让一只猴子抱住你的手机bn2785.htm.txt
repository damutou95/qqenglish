Tech designers are an imaginative bunch, but when it comes to commodity items like chargers and cable organizers, they tend toward the mundane.
科技产品设计者是一个颇有想象力的团体，但是在遇到充电器和绕线器等日常生活用品的问题时，他们的表现往往趋于平庸。
But the design company Felix has put a little whimsy into the ordinary phone stand and cable organizer.
然而，设计公司Felix在普通的手机座和绕线器上玩起了花样。
Felix has produced MonkeyOh, a simple $15 plastic phone stand that is shaped like a monkey and hangs from an electrical socket. It is goofy, but it is also useful.
Felix推出了一种叫做MonkeyOh的产品，它是一个售价15美元（约合90元人民币）的塑料手机座。它外形像猴子，可以挂在普通插座上。它有点傻，但却很有用。
Here’s how it works: Behind the monkey’s head is a receptacle sized for the charging block of an iPhone or iPod Touch. When you plug the charger into a wall socket, the monkey is suspended facing out. The USB plug goes in the monkey’s mouth, and excess cable wraps around the outside of the charger block.
它是这样用的：猴头后面是一个按iPhone或iPod Touch充电头的尺寸设计的托。当你把充电器插到墙上的插座里时，这个猴子会面朝外挂在墙上。USB可以从猴子的嘴里插进去，多余的线则可以缠绕在充电头的外面。
The phone nestles between MonkeyOh’s hands, with the screen out, so you can see alerts while the phone is charging.
手机则会被放在猴子的两手之间，屏幕朝外，这样手机在充电的时候，你还能看见相关提示。
The MonkeyOh is made of a thin and somewhat flexible plastic. I wouldn’t call the construction cheap, but I wouldn’t expect it to be impact-proof either.
制作MonkeyOh的材料是一种轻薄而较有弹性的塑料。虽然不能说是价格低廉，但我也不认为它能够抵御冲击。
Many average-size phones will fit MonkeyOh, including the Samsung Galaxy S3 and S4, but you will still need an iPhone charging block.
许多大小正常的手机都能使用MonkeyOh，其中包括三星(Samsun)的Galaxy S3和S4，但你还是需要一个iPhone充电头。
The MonkeyOh isn’t breakthrough technology on par with the discovery of the Higgs boson, but the elusive elementary particle doesn’t make me smile every time I pass one.
MonkeyOh并不像希格斯玻色子(Higgs boson)的发现那样具有突破意义，但我每次在经过这种神出鬼没的基本粒子时，脸上并不会浮现笑容。
