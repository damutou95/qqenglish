OUR ANCESTORS, hypnotized by gold, believed that by possessing it they could harness the strength and creative energy of the sun. Across cultures, there are legends devoted to its magical properties. A golden stool fell from the sky into the lap of the first Ashanti king in Ghana, while Danaë, the princess of Argos, conceived her heroic son Perseus with Zeus, who was disguised at the time as a fall of golden rain. The Russian artist Vadim Zakharov revisited that Greek myth earlier this year with a performance at the Venice Biennale.
我们的祖先迷恋金子，他们相信，拥有黄金就能掌握太阳的力量与创造的能源。各种文化中都有关于黄金魔力的传说。从天而降的黄金大便掉落在加纳第一位阿善堤王的膝头；宙斯令阿尔戈公主达娜厄怀上英雄珀修斯时，是隐身在在一阵黄金雨中；今年早些时候，俄罗斯艺术家瓦迪姆·扎哈罗夫(Vadim Zakharov)在威尼斯双年展上的一次表演中重现了这个古希腊神话。
Like daughters of Midas, models this season walked the runways bathed in a metallic glow. The classic extravagance at Dolce & Gabbana and the Klimtian patterns at L’Wren Scott looked to history and art for gilded inspiration, but other designers, like Giles Deacon and Dries Van Noten, added their own modern take on the glamour of gold, which remains the ultimate symbol of power and riches.
正如迈达斯（Midas，希腊神话中点石成金的国王——译注）的女儿们一样，本季的模特们也是沐浴着黄金的光辉走过秀台。杜嘉班纳(Dolce & Gabbana)的古典奢华风格与列文·斯科特(L’Wren Scott)的克里姆特式花纹都是从历史与艺术中寻觅镀金的灵感。而贾尔斯·迪肯(Giles Deacon)和德赖斯·范·诺顿(Dries Van Noten)等其他设计师一方面保持黄金在权力与财富方面的终极象征作用，一方面又为它添上带有自己个性色彩的现代化风格。
