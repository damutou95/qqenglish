With Batman and Superman battling each other for the first time in a feature film, here’s a look back at how the two have stacked up onscreen in live-action theatrical releases before “Batman v Superman.”
蝙蝠侠(Batman)和超人(Superman)正首次在一部长片里较量，所以让我们回顾一下，在《蝙蝠侠大战超人》(Batman v Superman)之前，这两位在实景真人电影中有过怎样的状况。
Number of ‘Batman’ movies: 8
《蝙蝠侠》电影的数量：8部
While Batman was serialized in films from 1943 and 1949, the first full-length feature adaptation of the DC Comics character was the 1966 film “Batman,” a movie version of the ’60s television series. It was followed by two “Batman” films directed by Tim Burton, two from Joel Schumacher and three directed by Christopher Nolan.
虽然从1943年至1949年，蝙蝠侠曾出现在系列电影里，但是由DC漫画(DC Comics)这个角色改编的第一部完整长度的正片是1966年的《蝙蝠侠》(Batman)，它是60年代《蝙蝠侠》电视剧的电影版。后来，蒂姆·伯顿(Tim Burton)导演了两部《蝙蝠侠》电影，乔尔·舒马赫(Joel Schumacher)导演了两部，克里斯托弗·诺兰(Christopher Nolan)导演了3部。
Number of ‘Superman’ movies: 7
《超人》电影的数量：7部
Superman was also serialized in movies, with the first feature coming in 1951 with “Superman and the Mole Men.” Next came “Superman” in 1978, then three more “Superman” movies in the ’80s. The franchise received a reboot in 2006 with “Superman Returns,” and another in 2013 with “Man of Steel.”
超人也曾出现在系列的电影里，第一部正片是1951年的《超人和鼹鼠人》(Superman and the Mole Men)。之后是1978年的《超人》，以及80年代的3部《超人》。2006年的《超人归来》(Superman Returns)和2013年的《超人：钢铁之躯》(Man of Steel)让这个漫画改编电影系列重现活力。
Number of live-action feature film ‘Batman’ actors: 5
实景真人正片中饰演蝙蝠侠的演员数量：5个
The 1966 feature had Adam West, from the television series, in the lead role. Michael Keaton took over in 1989 for two films. Then Val Kilmer and George Clooney each got their turns in the bat suit. Christian Bale took on the character for the three Christopher Nolan films. (Ben Affleck plays Batman in “Batman v Superman.”)
1966年的正片由电视剧中蝙蝠侠的饰演者亚当·韦斯特(Adam West)领衔主演。迈克尔·基顿(Michael Keaton)从1989年起主演了两部蝙蝠侠电影。之后，瓦尔·基尔默(Val Kilmer)和乔治·克鲁尼(George Clooney)都曾穿上蝙蝠装。克里斯蒂安·贝尔(Christian Bale)在克里斯托弗·诺兰导演的3部蝙蝠侠电影中饰演主角（本·阿弗莱克[Ben Affleck]在《蝙蝠侠大战超人》中饰演蝙蝠侠）。
Number of live-action feature film ‘Superman’ actors: 4
实景真人正片中饰演超人的演员数量：4个
George Reeves played him in “Superman and the Mole Men.” Christopher Reeve took on the role for the next four movies. Brandon Routh played the character in “Superman Returns” and Henry Cavill took over the role in “Man of Steel.” (Mr. Cavill reprises his role as Superman in “Batman v Superman.”)
乔治·里夫斯(George Reeves)在《超人和鼹鼠人》中饰演超人。克里斯托弗·里夫(Christopher Reeve)主演了接下来的4部超人电影。之后，布兰登·劳思(Brandon Routh)和亨利·卡维尔(Henry Cavill)分别在《超人归来》和《超人：钢铁之躯》中饰演这个角色（卡维尔在《蝙蝠侠大战超人》中继续饰演超人）。
Highest-grossing ‘Batman’ movie: The 2008 film “The Dark Knight” has the largest domestic gross on record, not adjusting for inflation, with $533.3 million according to figures from Box Office Mojo.
票房最高的《蝙蝠侠》电影：根据Box Office Mojo的数据，2008年的《黑暗骑士》(The Dark Knight)是美国国内票房收入最高的蝙蝠侠电影（不考虑通胀），高达5.333亿美元。
Highest-grossing ‘Superman’ movie: The 2013 film “Man of Steel” has the highest figure on record, with a nonadjusted gross of $291 million, according to figures from Box Office Mojo.
票房最高的《超人》电影：根据Box Office Mojo的数据，2013年的《超人：钢铁之躯》是美国国内票房收入最高的超人电影，达2.91亿美元（不考虑通胀）。
Batman is the bigger, richer franchise. But then, Bruce Wayne grew up in a manor and Clark Kent on a farm.
《蝙蝠侠》系列电影更宏大、更赚钱。不过要知道，布鲁斯·韦恩(Bruce Wayne)是在庄园里长大的，而克拉克·肯特(Clark Kent)是在农场里长大的。
