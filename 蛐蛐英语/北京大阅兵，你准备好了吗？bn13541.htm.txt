This year, China declared a new national holiday, Victory Day, on Sept. 3 to celebrate the 70th anniversary of the end of World War II. Beijing will stage a huge military parade through the center of the city, where 12,000 Chinese troops, joined by military units from 17 other countries, will march past leaders assembled at Tiananmen Gate as aircraft fly in formation overhead.
今年，中国宣布了一个新的全国性节日，把9月3日定为抗战胜利日，以庆祝第二次世界大战结束70周年。北京将在市中心举行一个巨大的阅兵仪式，将有1.2万名中国官兵参加，另有其他17个国家派军人参加，方队将从聚集在天安门城楼上的领导人面前列队经过，同时还有飞机编队在天上飞翔。
This is the first time that China is holding a military parade to commemorate what it calls the Chinese People’s Resistance Against Japanese Aggression and World Antifascist War since the founding of the People’s Republic in 1949. While the leaders of “all relevant countries” were invited, heads of state of Japan and major Western countries declined and will be represented by lower-level officials. Here is what to expect on Thursday:
这是自中华人民共和国1949年成立以来，中国首次举行纪念其称之为“中国人民抗日战争暨世界反法西斯战争”胜利的大阅兵，虽然“所有有关国家”的领导人都收到了邀请，但日本和西方主要国家的元首拒绝前往，只派了级别较低的官员作代表。下面是周四大阅兵将发生的事情：
When and Where
时间及地点
The parade will proceed from east to west on Changan Avenue past Tiananmen Square, starting at 10 a.m. and lasting about 70 minutes. At noon, there will be a reception for state leaders and foreign guests and in the evening, a gala, both in the Great Hall of the People.
方队将在长安街上从东向西行进，经过天安门广场，阅兵式上午10点开始，持续时间约70分钟。中午将有一个招待国家领导人和外宾的午餐，晚上还将举行文艺晚会，午餐和晚会的地点都是人民大会堂。
All this comes after a ceremony on Wednesday, at which President Xi Jinping will distribute commemorative medals to veterans of the war and early members of the Communist cause.
国家主席习近平将在周三的上述活动之前，主持一个纪念仪式，向抗战老兵和共产主义事业的早期成员颁发纪念章。
Who Will Be in the Parade?
将有那些部队参加阅兵？
There will be around 12,000 Chinese troops in 50 formations, including 11 infantry phalanxes, 27 vehicle and equipment formations, 10 aviation echelons and two motorized formations of veterans of the war against Japan.
将有大约1.2万名中国官兵组成的50个方队参加，其中包括11个徒步方队，27个车辆和设备方队，10个空中梯队，以及2个载有抗战老兵的机动车编队。
The average age of the veterans is 90. The oldest is 102. Many served in the Eighth Route Army, New Fourth Army, Northeast China Anti-Japanese United Army or the South China Guerrillas.
老兵们的平均年龄为90岁，其中最年长的102岁。许多人曾是八路军、新四军、中国东北抗日联合军或华南游击队的战士。
It will be the first time so many generals have participated, with 50 leading troops on foot. State news media reports that the generals have lost an average of 11 pounds during training.The parade will also feature a new female honor guard, consisting of 51 women from the army, navy and air force who will march alongside 156 male honor guards. According to state news media, the women are, on average, 20 years old and 5-foot-10.
这将是首次有多名将军参加的阅兵，有50名将军将步行带队。官方新闻媒体报道称，参加集训的将军体重平均掉了10斤。阅兵中还将有女兵参加仪仗队，她们是来自陆海空三军的51名女兵，将与由156名男兵组成的仪仗队同行。据官方媒体报道，这些女兵平均年龄20岁，平均身高1.78米。
Seventeen countries are contributing around 1,000 troops.
还有来自17个国家的约1000名部队官兵。
Of these, 11 have sent formations of about 75 people each: Belarus, Cuba, Egypt, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mexico, Mongolia, Pakistan, Serbia, Tajikistan and Russia. Six countries have sent smaller teams of about seven people each: Afghanistan, Cambodia, Fiji, Laos, Vanuatu and Venezuela.
其中有11个国家各派了一只约75人的方队，它们是白俄罗斯、古巴、埃及、哈萨克斯坦、吉尔吉斯斯坦、墨西哥、蒙古、巴基斯坦、塞尔维亚、塔吉克斯坦，以及俄罗斯。有6个国家各派了一个约7人的小队，它们是阿富汗、柬埔寨、斐济、老挝、瓦努阿图和委内瑞拉。
What Weapons Will Be on Display?
将展示哪些武器？
The parade will provide a showcase for China’s weaponry, 84 percent of which, organizers say, will be seen in public for the first time.
检阅军队将为展示中国武器装备提供一个窗口，组织者称，所展示的武器中有84％将是首次与公众见面。
Foreign military analysts are especially interested in the YJ-18, a long-range supersonic antiship missile. Some American experts say they believe this could quadruple the range of current antiship cruise missiles fired from China’s submarines.
外国军事分析人士对YJ-18远程超音速反舰导弹非常感兴趣。一些美国专家说，他们认为这种远程导弹可能会让中国潜艇发射的反舰巡航导弹的射程翻两番。
The Chinese have said the DF-16, a medium-range ballistic missile first launched in 2009 but not seen in public, will be in the parade.Military analysts are watching to see if the DF-41 intercontinental missile, which can carry multiple warheads and was tested this year, makes an appearance.
中国曾表示，2009年首次推出的中程弹道导弹DF-16也将出现在阅兵式上，这种导弹以前没有公开露过面。军事分析人士在观望洲际导弹DF-41是否会在阅兵式上出现，这种可携带多弹头的导弹已于今年进行了测试。
Seen during a rehearsal for the parade was the DF-21D antiship ballistic missile, the so-called carrier killer that the Chinese have developed to prevent United States carrier groups from participating in a conflict in the Taiwan Strait. In 2009, the United States Naval Institute said that the system would give China the ability to destroy an American supercarrier in one strike.
曾在阅兵排练中看到的是DF-21D反舰弹道导弹，既所谓的航母杀手，这是中国为防止美国航母舰队参与台湾海峡冲突而研发的。2009年，美国海军研究所曾表示，这种武器将赋予中国一弹摧毁美国超级航母的能力。
Nearly 200 aircraft are expected to take part. Rehearsals featured J-15 fighter jets and Z-19 attack helicopters, with aircraft flying in formation to form the number 70.
还有近200架飞机预计也将参加阅兵。参加排练的主角是J-15喷气式战斗机和Z-19攻击直升机，飞机将以形成数字70的编队飞行。
Music
阅兵式音乐
The People’s Liberation Army is also fielding more than 1,000 musicians and 1,000 singers to perform nearly 30 musical pieces.
中国人民解放军还将派出1000多名乐队成员及1000多名歌手表演近30支歌曲。
These include: “The Song of the Anti-Japanese War Military Political School,” “Protecting the Yellow River,” “On Taihang Mountain” and “The People’s Army Troops are Loyal to the Party.”
这些歌曲包括：《抗日军政大学校歌》、《保护黄河》、《在太行山上》，以及《人民军队忠于党》。
