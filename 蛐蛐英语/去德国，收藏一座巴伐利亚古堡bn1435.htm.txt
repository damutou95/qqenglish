A 12th-CENTURY BAVARIAN CASTLE SURROUNDED BY A MOAT AND 27 ACRES
待售房产：一座12世纪的巴伐利亚古堡，占地27英亩（约10.9公顷），有一条护城河环绕。
5,700,000 EUROS ($7.4 MILLION)
价格：570万欧元（或740万美元，约合人民币4722.56万元）
Perched on a steep cliff overlooking the Altmühl river valley in Germany’s Bavaria state, this 27-acre property includes an eight-bedroom limestone castle that dates to the middle of the 12th century, when it was constructed by knights. Known as Burg Kipfenberg, the medieval castle was later owned by a church bishop and expanded over the centuries. The 18-room castle was completely reconstructed from 1915 to 1925 by the German architect and castle expert Bodo Ebhardt, who is largely responsible for the castle’s silhouette of towers with hipped and gabled roofs of tile. The castle later fell into disrepair.
这片占地27英亩（约10.9公顷）的房产，坐落在一座俯瞰德国巴伐利亚州阿尔特米尔河谷(Altmühl river valley)的陡峭山崖上，其间有一栋带有八间卧室的石灰岩城堡，历史可追溯到12世纪中期，兴建者是几名骑士。这栋中世纪古堡名为“基普芬贝格堡”(Burg Kipfenberg)，后由一位教堂主教掌管，并在数百年间逐渐扩建。在1915至1925年间，这栋已经拥有18个房间的城堡曾由德国建筑师兼城堡专家博多·埃巴特(Bodo Ebhardt)彻底重建。他主要负责构筑城堡群塔的轮廓，再现了铺着瓦片的四坡屋顶与人字屋顶。然而，这座城堡后来还是陷入了年久失修的境地。
“About 20 years ago, the current owners began a massive renovation of the previously neglected castle,” said Matthias Helzel, an owner of Vermittlung historischer Immobilien, a real estate agency that specializes in historic buildings and has the listing for Burg Kipfenberg. “These days, the castle is well-maintained in excellent condition and has its own caretaker.”
“大约20年前，目前的业主开始对这座此前一直疏于照管的城堡进行大规模翻修，”马赛厄斯·黑尔策尔(Matthias Helzel)说，“现在，这座城堡被维护得非常好，还有自己的维护人员。”黑尔策尔是专业代理历史建筑并挂牌出售基普芬贝格堡的房地产公司Vermittlung historischer Immobilien的所有人之一。
Most of the property’s 27 acres are meadows, pasture and forest. The castle is encircled by a dry moat; a wooden bridge leads to the heavy wooden and iron doors of the castle gate.  Limestone walls enclose the castle, a caretaker’s house, stables, three guesthouses and a small museum, all on six acres.  The buildings are included in the property’s asking price except for the museum and one prefabricated house, which cost an additional 1 million and 500,000 euros, Mr. Helzel said, or about $1.3 million and $650,000. About three-quarters of the castle furnishings are also covered in the asking price, and the rest are negotiable, according to Bernd Neuhäuser, who also owns Vermittlung historischer Immobilien. The castle has nearly 9,500 square feet of living space and includes a gothic hall, a knights hall, library, private chapel, two kitchens, dining room and living room, Mr. Helzel said. The caretaker’s house and three guesthouses encompass an additional 4,000 square feet.
这处房产占据的27英亩土地上，大部分覆盖着草地、牧场和森林。城堡被一条干涸的护城河环绕；一座木桥通往城堡那扇木材与铁制成的厚重大门。在石灰岩的围墙之内，有城堡主体、一栋看门人的屋舍，一个马厩，三间客用房和一座小博物馆，一共占地6英亩（约2.43公顷）。这些建筑几乎都包含在售价之中，除了那座博物馆和一栋预制板房，需要另外收取100万欧元（约合人民币831.82万元）和50万欧元（约合人民币415.91万元）的费用，或分别约合130万和65万美元，黑尔策尔介绍。又据Vermittlung historischer Immobilien地产公司的另一位所有人贝恩德·诺伊豪泽尔(Bernd Neuhäuser)说，城堡中的家具约有四分之三也包含在售价之中，其余部分还可商议。这座城堡有将近9500平方英尺（约882.6平方米）的生活空间，包括一个哥特式门厅、一个骑士厅、一间图书馆、一间私人祈祷堂、两个厨房、一间餐厅和一间客厅，黑尔策尔介绍说。看门人的屋舍和三间客用房的占地共计4000平方英尺（约371.6平方米）。
The castle’s main doors open to a long stone hall with rough-hewed limestone on either side — the result of a large limestone outcropping that rooms were built around, Mr. Neuhäuser said. A wooden stairway ascends to the living quarters, which include a lounge with stone fireplace, heavy wooden ceiling beams and a natural limestone wall. The lounge adjoins a dining area with a domed ceiling painted with frescos from the early 20th century. One of two kitchens has wooden cabinets, and the other has a large red island with a sink and oven that was custom-made in France, Mr. Helzel said. The castle has central oil heating, and the guesthouses have underfloor heating.
古堡的正门通往一条石砌长廊，长廊两侧都是切面粗糙的石灰岩石——这是在突起的巨型石灰岩周围建造房间的结果，诺伊豪泽尔说。一段木质楼梯向上通往生活区，那里包括一间有石砌壁炉、厚重木梁与天然石灰岩墙壁的会客室。这间会客室连着一间餐厅，餐厅房顶上绘有20世纪早期的壁画。黑尔策尔说，在那两间厨房中，一间装有木制橱柜，另一间则带一座红色的大型岛台，以及一个水池和在法国定做的炉子。城堡中安装了燃油式中央供暖系统，客用房配备的则是地板采暖系统。
Burg Kipfenberg overlooks the town of Kipfenberg, with about 5,700 people, and is midway between the cities of Nuremberg to the north and Munich to the south. The area is surrounded by the Altmühltal Nature Park, which is about 1,120 square miles and offers hiking, climbing, cycling and boating. An international train station is in the city of Ingolstadt, which is about a half-hour’s drive. It takes about an hour to get to the international airports in Nuremberg and Munich, Mr. Helzel said.
基普芬贝格堡俯瞰着约有5700名居民的基普芬贝格小镇，位于北部的纽伦堡(Nuremberg)与南部的慕尼黑(Munich)这两座城市之间，被阿尔特米尔塔尔自然公园(Altmühltal Nature Park)环抱。该公园占地面积约为1120平方英里（约2900平方公里），提供了徒步、登山、骑自行车与划船的场地。一座国际火车站位于因戈尔施塔特市(Ingolstadt)，开车约半小时可到；黑尔策尔说，到达纽伦堡(Nuremberg)国际机场，大约需要一小时车程。
MARKET OVERVIEW
市场概况
The conservative lending practices of German banks have bolstered home prices, which have continued to climb despite the global economic downturn, brokers said. The market also has been reinforced by a strong economy that has drawn many immigrant workers, and the relatively low unemployment in Bavaria, according to brokers. In some parts of Munich, the capital of the Bavarian state, home prices have jumped 30 to 40 percent in the last three or four years, said Michael Lindmair, a Bavaria-based real estate agent with Engel & Völkers.
德国银行保守的借贷政策已经抬高了住宅价格，而且尽管全球经济已开始下滑，德国的房价还将持续上涨，有房地产经纪人这样说。经纪人称，这一市场涨势，还得益于一股强劲的经济发展势头，它吸引了许多移民工人，使巴伐利亚州(Bavaria)保持了相对较低的失业率。在巴伐利亚州首府慕尼黑(Munich)的部分地区，房屋价格在过去三、四年中已经上涨了30%至40%，巴伐利亚当地一家房地产公司Engel & Völkers的经纪人麦克·林德梅尔(Michae Lindmaire)说。
Germany’s housing market, though, has little bearing on the niche market for castles, Mr. Neuhäuser said. He estimated that Germany has about 5,000 castles, which range in size from about 4,000 square feet to more than 21,500 square feet of living area, with the average being about 7,500 to 8,500 square feet. Prices range dramatically, but the larger privately owned renovated castles might start at 10 million euros, or $12.9 million, and go as high as 19 million euros, or $25 million.
不过诺伊豪泽尔说，德国的总体房屋市场对于较为独特的城堡市场并无多少影响。他估计德国有大约5000座城堡，使用面积从4000平方英尺（约371.6平方米）到21500平方英尺（约1997.4平方米）不等，平均在7500平方英尺（约696.8平方米）到8500平方英尺（约789.7平方米）间。城堡的价格区间跨度极大，而面积较大且翻修过的私有城堡，可能以1000万欧元（约合人民币8332.11万元）或1290万美元起售；其价格最高可达1900万欧元（约合人民币1.583亿元），或者2500万美元。
Only about a third of Germany’s castles are fully renovated, which is a major selling point for buyers, Mr. Neuhäuser said.
诺伊豪泽尔说，在德国的城堡当中，只有大约三分之一曾被整体翻修，而这对买家而言正是一大卖点。
WHO BUYS IN GERMANY
谁在德国买房？
Along with Berlin and Hamburg, Munich is among Germany’s most attractive cities for foreigners, said Mr. Lindmair, whose clients include many Russians, Americans and Britons. The euro crisis and the relative strength of the British pound have led to the recent influx of British buyers, he said. In the market for castles, foreigners typically account for about half of the buyers, and they come from all over the world, Mr. Neuhäuser said. “We see Russians, Chinese, Turkish people, Dutch, Italians — it’s a mixture,” he said. There recently have been many Russians in the market for German castles, but typically they are looking for castles in the cities, which are hard to find, he said.
对外国买家来说，慕尼黑与柏林和汉堡一样，也是德国最有吸引力的城市之一，林德梅尔说，他的客户中有很多是俄罗斯人、美国人和英国人。他说，欧元危机与英镑的相对强势，使得最近有大量英国买家涌入。而具体到城堡买卖市场，外国人士一般占到买家总人数的大约一半，他们来自世界各地，诺伊豪泽尔说。“我们遇到过俄国人、中国人、土耳其人、荷兰人、意大利人——哪儿的人都有，”他说。最近有很多俄国买家在看德国城堡，但他们通常都想要城市里的城堡，这是很难找的，他说。
“The cities have big castles, which are typically in state ownership,” he said. “Privately owned castles are in the countryside.”
“城市里有一些大城堡，但一般都属国有资产，”他说，“而私有的城堡都在乡间。”
BUYING BASICS
基本购买条件
There are no restrictions on foreign buyers in Germany, though a notary must be hired for sales transactions. The buyer can generally expect to pay an additional 10 percent of the home’s sales price in notary fees, state tax and real estate broker’s fees, Mr. Neuhäuser said. Broker fees are typically about 6 percent, and are divided between the buyer and seller in Bavaria.
德国对外国买家没有限制，不过必须聘请一位公正人参与交易。买家一般有望额外支付房屋售价的10%作为公证费、国税、和房地产代理费，诺伊豪泽尔说。代理费一般约为6%，在巴伐利亚州由买家和卖家共同分担。

TAXES AND FEES
税收与费用
The total annual cost to run Burg Kipfenberg is 100,000 euros, or about $130,000, which includes the property taxes, the caretaker’s salary, insurance and heating, Mr. Neuhäuser said.
诺伊豪泽尔说，基普芬贝格堡每年的总运营成本为10万欧元（约合人民币83万元），或13万美元，其中包括房产税、维护人员的薪水、保险及供暖费。
