Fans come in all shapes and attire.
球迷们来了，穿着千姿百态、五光十色的衣装。
A company based in London partial to “fancy dress,” as they say in the British Isles, has taken fan wear to a place few fans would dare to tread: the unitard. Think Spider-Man meets the American Outlaws (though only the svelte need apply ... please).
伦敦一家对“奇装异服”情有独钟的公司，把球迷装的设计带到了一个鲜有球迷敢于尝试的境界：从头到脚的弹力紧身衣。想象一下蜘蛛侠和“美国亡命徒”合二为一的样子吧（不过胖子不宜……）。
For the World Cup, the Morph Costume Co. offers the ultimate in fan gear: flag suits of 14 counties (including several teams that will not be in Brazil.
为了迎接世界杯，Morph Costume Co.公司推出了终极球迷装：14个国家队的国旗装（其中包括几个不参加本届世界杯的球队）。
The suits, which sell for about $67 direct from the company, are now out there for World Cup fans from the United States, Mexico, Australia, France, England, Italy, Germany, the Netherlands and Spain. (The suits for nonparticipants are from Wales, Ireland, Scotland and Canada.)
直接从该公司购买这种服装的价格大约为67美元（约合417.3元人民币），现在，来自美国、墨西哥、澳大利亚、法国、英国、意大利、德国、荷兰和西班牙的世界杯球迷可以购买自己国家的国旗装了。（这一系列的国旗装包括的非参赛国家是威尔士，爱尔兰，苏格兰和加拿大。）
