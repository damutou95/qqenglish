A COTSWOLD COTTAGE-STYLE HOME IN VICTORIA, BRITISH COLUMBIA
加拿大不列颠哥伦比亚省维多利亚市的一栋科茨沃尔德木屋风格的住宅
1,699,000 CANADIAN DOLLARS ($1.291 MILLION) 
169.9万加元（129.1万美元，约合人民币814万元）
This three-story Cotswold Cottage-style home in Victoria, on Vancouver Island, offers mountain and sea views from its south-facing back deck. Dating to 1936, the 4,930-square-foot house, with five bedrooms and four and a half baths on a half-acre lot, has stucco exterior walls and cedar shingles and is set into a slight incline.
这栋三层楼的科茨沃尔德木屋式住宅位于加拿大温哥华岛(Vancouver Island)上的维多利亚市，从屋后朝南的平台上可以看见山景和海景。这栋始建于1936年、面积4930平方英尺（约458平方米）的房屋占地半英亩（约2023平方米），带五间卧室和四间半浴室，有灰泥粉饰的外墙和雪松木叠瓦屋顶，坐落在一个和缓的斜坡上。
The main entrance, at midlevel, opens to a foyer with parquet floors and a staircase on the left. On the right are a bedroom with an en-suite bathroom, next to a den and a powder room that could be converted together into a sixth bedroom. On the left is the kitchen, which was updated in 2007 with white painted cabinets, granite countertops and stainless-steel appliances, including a Viking range and a Sub-Zero refrigerator. Beyond the foyer are a living room, a dining room and a family room, all of which abut a wraparound deck of about 1,140 square feet with a brick wood-burning fireplace. The living room has a working gas fireplace of marble. Floors on the main level are oak. The cottage has original leaded windows and other details.
该房屋的主入口位于中间层，这里通往一间铺着拼花木地板、左边有一段楼梯的门厅。门厅右边是一间带浴室的卧房，旁边有一间小房和一间化妆室，这两间房可以打通，改建成第六间卧室。门厅的左边是厨房，曾于2007年翻修过，有白漆橱柜、花岗岩台面和一些不锈钢电器，其中包括一台Viking炉灶和一台Sub-zero冰箱。这间门厅外有一间客厅、一间餐厅和一间家庭娱乐室，这几间房都紧邻一处面积1140平方英尺（约106平方米）、带一座砖砌燃木壁炉的环绕式平台。客厅里有一座可工作的燃气大理石壁炉。主楼层的地面铺着橡木地板。这栋小木屋保留了几扇原有的有铅玻璃窗和其他一些细节。
The top floor of the home has three bedrooms, including the master, which has an en-suite bathroom, walk-in closet and a gas fireplace. The other two bedrooms share a bathroom. The floors upstairs are fir, and the bathrooms have in-floor heating.
这栋房屋的顶层有三间卧室，其中包括主卧室，这间卧室带一间浴室、一个步入式衣柜和一座燃气壁炉。另外两间卧室共用一间浴室。楼上的地面铺着冷杉木地板，浴室带地暖。
The lower level of the house has a large open expanse, set up as a combination games room, rec room and media room, that opens to two patios. Also on the lower level are a bedroom/den, a bathroom, a laundry and storage rooms. Furniture is not included in the asking price but is negotiable.
这栋房屋的底层有一大片开敞式的扩建区域，那里包括组合游戏室、娱乐室和媒体室，可通往两处露台。底层还有一间卧室/小房、一间浴室、一间洗衣房和几间储藏室。该房屋要价不含家具在内，但可以商榷。
The house, which is in the historic Rockland neighborhood of Victoria, has no garage, but there is off-street parking for four cars. The neighborhood, which is about a 10-minute walk from the city center, is largely residential and has good public transportation, said Scott Piercy, an agent with Engel & Völkers Victoria, which has the listing. The home is close to Government House, the residence of the lieutenant governor of British Columbia, along with Pemberton Park and excellent public and private schools, he said. The Victorian-era Craigdarroch Castle, a national historic site, and the Art Gallery of Greater Victoria are nearby. The closest grocery shopping is in the adjacent neighborhood of Fairfield, about a 10-minute walk. It’s roughly a 40-minute drive to Victoria’s international airport. Downtown Vancouver can also be reached from downtown Victoria by floatplane in about 35 minutes, Mr. Piercy said.
这栋坐落在维多利亚市罗克兰(Rockland)历史社区的房屋不带车库，但路边可停放四辆车。代理这栋房屋的维多利亚Engel & Völkers房地产公司的代理人斯科特·皮尔斯(Scott Piercy)说，这个距离市中心约10分钟步行路程的社区大体上是住宅区，公共交通十分便利。他说，这栋房屋靠近省督府(Government House)，即不列颠哥伦比亚省副省督的官邸，旁边还有彭伯顿公园(Pemberton Park)和几所出色的公立与私立学校。国家历史名胜、维多利亚时代的魁达洛古堡(Craigdarroch Castle)和大维多利亚区美术馆(Art Gallery of Greater Victoria)就在附近。距离最近的购物区在旁边的费尔菲尔德(Fairfield)，步行大约10分钟可达。从这里开车大约40分钟可到维多利亚国际机场。皮尔斯说，这里还可以搭乘飞艇，大约35分钟可从维多利亚市中心到达温哥华市中心。
MARKET OVERVIEW
市场概况
Victoria, a city of roughly 80,000 in a greater metropolitan area of about 345,000 on Vancouver Island, has had a robust single-family housing market in the last several years, said Bill Ethier, the president and managing broker of Royal LePage Coast Capital Realty.
维多利亚是一座约有8万人的城市，位于温哥华岛上一个约有34.5万人的大都市区。据皇家勒帕热海岸资本房地产公司(Royal LePage Coast Capital Realty)的总裁兼主管经纪人比尔·埃西亚(Bill Ethier)说，过去几年中，该市一直保持着稳健的独栋住宅市场。
“In single-family dwellings, we’re seeing price increases as high as 15 percent year-over-year in some areas,” he said. “In the market in general, if we add in condominiums and townhouses, we’re looking at increases of 4 percent.”
“在独栋住宅市场中，我们看见价格在某些区域同比增长高达15%，”他说。“而在整个市场中，如果算上合作公寓和联排别墅的话，我们会看到4%的价格增涨。”
Home prices did fall by about 10 percent to 15 percent in the global real estate crisis of 2008, but they recovered relatively quickly, brokers said. Inventory is low, and the health of the housing market in the much larger city of Vancouver on the mainland, which has about 600,000 residents in a greater metropolitan area of about 2.4 million, is pushing up Victoria’s home prices, Mr. Piercy said.
据一些经纪人说，在2008年的全球房地产危机中，该市的住宅价格的确下跌了大约10%至15%，但后来恢复得相对较快。皮尔斯说，目前该市的房屋存量较低，而且，位于内陆、面积更大且拥有大约60万居民的温哥华市（位于一个人口约240万的大都市区中），正以其住宅市场的健康发展推动着维多利亚市的住宅价格不断上涨。
In October this year, the average price of a single-family home in central Victoria was 608,200 Canadian dollars, or about $462,232; that was a 9.4 percent increase over the average price in October 2014 of 555,900 Canadian dollars, or about $422,484, said Julie Rust, a sales representative with Newport Realty.
据纽波特房地产公司(Newport Realty)的一位销售代表朱莉·拉斯特(Julie Rust)说，今年十月，维多利亚市中心区的独栋住宅均价为60.82万加元（约462232美元，人民币约291万元）；比2014年10月的均价55.59万加元（约422484美元，人民币约266万元）增长了9.4%。
“We’re now definitely a seller’s market,” she said. “There is much more competition and multiple offers fueled by low interest rates, increased demand and the sudden discovery of this wonderful community.”
“我们目前绝对是卖方市场，”他说。“而且由于较低的利率、激增的需求以及这个优质社区的忽然发现，市场上出现了更多的买家竞争与数倍的报价。”
The priciest housing market on Vancouver Island, Mr. Ethier said, is Oak Bay, a municipality in the greater Victoria area, which includes the affluent neighborhood of Uplands. Single-family homes in Uplands can range from one million Canadian dollars, or about $760,000, for a tear-down, up to 10 or 15 million Canadian, or $7.6 million to $11.4 million, he said.
埃西亚说，温哥华岛上价格最高的住宅市场是大维多利亚区的自治市橡树湾(OakBay)，其中包括富庶的Uplands社区。他说在该社区中，独栋住宅的价格从100万加元（约76万美元，人民币约478.9万元）的危楼，至1000万加元（约760万美元，人民币4789万元）或1500万加元（约1140万美元，人民币约7183.8万元）不等。
Within the city of Victoria, the most desirable neighborhoods are Fairfield and Rockland, Mr. Ethier said. “If people are looking for the equivalent of blue-chip stocks in real estate, Fairfield, Rockland, Oak Bay are where home prices are the first to go up and the last to go down,” he said.
埃西亚说，在维多利亚市，最抢手的社区是费尔菲尔德和罗克兰社区。“如果人们要找房地产中的蓝筹股，那就得去费尔菲尔德、罗克兰和橡树湾等社区，那是房价涨起来最快跌 起来最慢的几个地方，”他说。
WHO BUYS IN VICTORIA
谁在维多利亚市买房
Neither the government nor the real estate industry regularly collects data on foreign homeownership on Vancouver Island, since the numbers of foreign homeowners are small, brokers said. Most home buyers are Canadian, typically from British Columbia, though there are also retirees from Alberta, Saskatchewan and Ontario, Ms. Rust said. United States citizens, the largest group of foreign home buyers, diminished after the global real estate crisis, but have begun to return in larger numbers with the strong United States dollar, brokers said.
据一些经纪人说，无论是政府还是地产业都不会经常收集温哥华岛上外国业主的身份信息，因为这里的外国业主数量很少。拉斯特说，大多数住宅购买者都是加拿大人，一般来自不列颠哥伦比亚省，不过也有一些来自艾伯塔省(Alberta)、萨斯喀彻温省(Saskatchewan) 和安大略省(Ontario)的退休者。据经纪人说，外国买家中的最大群体是美国公民，他们在全球房地产危机之后数量骤减，但随着美元走势强劲又开始增多。
There are also growing numbers of Chinese home buyers, as well as a small percentage of European home buyers, Mr. Piercy said.
皮尔斯说，中国买家的数量也在不断增长，还有一小部分是欧洲的买家。
BUYING BASICS
购买基本条件
Foreigners face no restrictions when buying homes in Canada, though financing a property with Canadian lenders can be harder, typically requiring a 35 percent down payment, while Canadians can buy with as little as 5 percent down, Mr. Ethier said.
埃西亚说，外国买家在加拿大买房不受限制，不过找加拿大银行办理抵押贷款可能会困难一些，一般要求支付35%的首期款，而加拿大人只需支付5%的首期款就可以了。
Transaction fees for home buyers include legal fees _ — buyers generally hire a lawyer — which are typically around 800 to 1,500 Canadian dollars, and property transfer tax, which is 1 percent on the first 200,000 Canadian dollars and 2 percent on the balance, brokers said. If this house sold for the listing price, the property transfer tax would be about 32,000 Canadian dollars, or around $24,320, Mr. Piercy said.
据一些经纪人说，房产购买者的交易费用中包括一项通常为800至1500加元（约合人民币3831元至7184元）的律师费——买家通常要聘请一位律师——和一项按前20万加元（约合人民币96万元）收取1%、其余部分收取2%计算的房地产转让税。皮尔斯说，如果该房屋以要价出售，其房地产转让税约为3.2万加元（约合人民币15万元），或24320美元。
