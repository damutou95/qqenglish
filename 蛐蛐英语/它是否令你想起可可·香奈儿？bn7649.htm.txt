Last week, the renovated Georgian Restaurant opened at Harrods in London, with furnishings inspired by Coco Chanel. “With this collection, I asked myself, ‘How do I bring elegance into today’s world without looking dated?’ ” said Christopher Guy Harrison, a British furniture maker who designs under the name Christopher Guy, and who based the collection, called Mademoiselle, on pieces from Ms. Chanel’s suite at the Ritz Hotel in Paris. Soft contours, metallic glimmers and lush velvet are features of his seven chair and five banquette styles. The collection also includes mirrors, mosaics and a copper statue of a Georgian aristocrat.
上周（7月20日当周——译注），重新装修的乔治亚餐厅(Georgian Restaurant)在伦敦哈罗兹(Harrods)百货商店隆重开业，店内家具用品的设计灵感皆来自于可可·香奈儿(Coco Chanel)。“在考虑该系列的设计风格时，我问自己‘怎样才能把优雅带入当代世界，而不显得过时？’”英国家具制造商克里斯多夫·盖伊·哈里森(Christopher Guy Harrison)说。他在设计工作中使用的名字是克里斯多夫·盖伊(Christopher Guy)，而他这一系列名为“法国小姐”(Mademoiselle)的室内作品，都是基于巴黎丽兹酒店(Ritz Hotel)里香奈儿女士所穿套装中的几件衣服而设计的。其柔美的轮廓、金属般的光泽以及奢华的天鹅绒面料，凸显了他这次设计的“7”字椅与五人长沙发的风格。该系列的家具作品还包括几面镜子、一些马赛克拼图以及一尊乔治亚时期的铜质贵族雕像。
Several of the pieces, including the settee, ($16,410), are on view at the Las Vegas Market, which opened on Sunday, and can be bought from Mr. Harrison’s Las Vegas showroom. Information: christopherguy.com.
