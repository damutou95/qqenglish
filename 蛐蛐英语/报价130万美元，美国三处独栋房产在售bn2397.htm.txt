SETTING: Madison is a borough of 16,000 people 30 miles from New York. This 1903 foursquare is on a two-lane road that runs through town, across from Drew University. The borough is also home to Fairleigh Dickinson University. The neighborhood is a mix of older and newer houses, some dating back to the early 20th century, when the area was the summer home of wealthy families. Downtown, under a mile away from this house, has small shops and restaurants, blocks lined with iron benches and old-fashioned street lamps. There’s also a New Jersey Transit train stop.
周边环境：麦迪逊是一个1.6万人的行政区，距离纽约30英里（约48公里）。这栋1903年的别墅位于一条穿市中心而过的双车道公路边，对面就是杜尔大学(Drew University)。该行政区还是费尔里·狄金生大学(Fairleigh Dickinson University)的所在地。附近新、旧房屋混杂分布，其中有些房屋可追溯到20世纪早期，那时候，这里是有钱人的避暑胜地。距离这栋房屋不到1英里（约1.6公里）的市中心，有一些小商店和餐馆，铁质长椅和老式街灯在一个个街区沿途一字排开。这里还有一个新泽西运输公司(New Jersey Transit)的火车站。
INDOORS: The three-story house has retained most of its original details, including chestnut built-ins and woodwork, leaded stained-glass windows, bead-board ceilings and oak floors. The first floor has two parlors, one on either side of the foyer, both with wood-burning fireplaces with the original glazed-tile surrounds. The kitchen has granite counters, custom cabinets and a pressed-tin backsplash. A butler’s pantry with a wet bar connects it to the dining room, which has built-in china cabinets. Reproduction and period wallpaper was put up during renovations.
室内环境：这栋三层的房屋大体上保持了原样，其中包括栗木的嵌壁式家具和木工制品，含铅的彩色玻璃窗，隔板吊顶和橡木地板。一楼有两间起居室，门厅两侧一边一个，这两间起居室中都有燃木壁炉，炉周贴着原有的釉面砖。厨房里有一些花岗岩的料理台、定做的橱柜，和一块锡膜(pressed-tin)挡板。一间带酒吧的餐具室连着餐厅，餐厅里有嵌壁式瓷器橱柜。再生墙纸和复古墙纸是翻修期间贴上的。
Bedrooms are on the second and third floors. The master, on the second floors, has a fireplace. Both of the upstairs bathrooms have claw-foot tubs. Parts of the basement are finished, with a recreation room, a wine cellar and storage rooms. The property’s carriage house has been converted into a large living area with an original glass-front trophy cabinet and wainscoting throughout.
几间卧室都在二楼和三楼。二楼的主卧室里有一个壁炉。楼上的两间浴室里，都带有四足浴缸。地下室的部分地方已经完工，包括一间娱乐室、一个酒窖和几间储藏室。这处房产的马车房已被改成一个大面积的生活区，里面有一个翻修前就有的玻璃门纪念品橱柜，整个区域内都有护墙板。

SETTING: University Park is an inner-ring suburb of Dallas, about six miles from downtown. 周边环境：大学园在达拉斯的内环郊区，距市中心大约6英里（约10公里）。这栋采用石材立面的1926年乡村别墅，位于一段短短的断路旁，这条路绕过达拉斯乡村俱乐部(Dallas Country Club)的一侧，与几座市立公园相距两个街区。此居住区内也是新、旧房屋混杂分布。当地的公立学校很有声望，比如《新闻周刊》(Newsweek)曾把高地公园中学(Highland Park High School)列为全美首屈一指的学校之一。

The cabinets are hand-painted. In a small hallway off the dining room, there’s a wet bar. Also on the first floor is a library, a den and a sunroom with a bowed wood-paneled ceiling and French doors opening to the backyard. The entry-level master bedroom is part of a suite with a walk-in closet and a bathroom with a separate shower and tub, updated in 2002. The other two bedrooms are upstairs.

SETTING: Bloomfield Hills is a wooded suburb about 20 miles outside of Detroit. The houses were designed by Harold Turner, a builder for Frank Lloyd Wright. (Turner’s personal residence was next door.) Grocery stores, restaurants and other amenities are within a mile, in the city’s small downtown.
周边环境：布隆菲尔德山在距底特律(Detroit)市以外大约20英里（约32公里）处，是一个坐落于林地间的乡村。这栋建于1950年的房屋属于上世纪中叶建造的一片现代住宅区，地处一条断路的乡间小道旁，俯瞰着吉尔伯特湖(Gilbert Lake)，这是该地区内星罗分布的几片湖泊之一。这些住宅，都是由弗兰克·劳埃德·赖特(Frank Lloyd Wright)的建造商哈罗德·特纳(Harold Turner)设计的（特纳的私人住所就在隔壁）。杂货店、餐馆和其它设施都位于这座城市的一小片中心区，在1英里内（约1.61公里）。

室内环境：这栋房屋是一个单层的砖混石灰石建筑，带有斜坡屋顶和几面落地窗，有的窗户可以看见湖景。这栋房屋在2004年到2006年间由一家在当地经营了50多年的家庭式建筑公司Young & Young翻修过。

Like many of the rooms, this room opens to a patio. The master bedroom, at the other end of the house, has partial lake views and its own patio.
