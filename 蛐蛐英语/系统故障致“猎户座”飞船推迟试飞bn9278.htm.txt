NASA postponed the first test flight of its Orion spacecraft on Thursday morning when a glitch developed in the launch rocket’s fuel system.
美国国家航空航天局(NASA)周四上午推迟了“猎户座”宇宙飞船(Orion)的首次试飞，原因是运载火箭的燃料系统出现故障。
Officials said they would try again on Friday morning, although the weather was expected to deteriorate at the launching site at Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida.
官方表示，他们将在周五上午再次进行尝试。不过，佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角空军基地(Cape Canaveral Air Force Station)发射现场的天气状况预计将会恶化。
The $375-million flight is intended to test re-entry systems for the Orion capsule, which is designed to eventually carry astronauts beyond Earth orbit, perhaps to the moon or Mars. No one will be on board for the test, which will consist of two orbits followed by splashdown in the Pacific Ocean off Baja California.
此次试飞估计耗资3.75亿美元（约合23亿元人民币），打算对“猎户座”太空舱的重返大气层系统进行测试。这座太空舱的设计用途是最终搭载宇航员离开地球轨道，接下来可能会前往月球或火星。这次试飞不会搭载宇航员，飞船将绕轨道飞行两圈，然后落入墨西哥下加利福尼亚州附近的太平洋海域。
