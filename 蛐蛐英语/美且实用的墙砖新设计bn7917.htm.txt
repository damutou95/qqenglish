The bridge between design and real-life application can sometimes be shaky. So when Paul Schatz, an interior designer, teamed with New Ravenna Mosaics to develop glass tiles, he said he wanted tiles that were easy on the eyes and on the user as well.
衔接设计构思与实际应用的那座桥梁，有时可能并不稳固。所以，当室内设计师保罗·沙兹(Paul Schatz)与纽拉文纳马赛克公司(New Ravenna Mosaics)合作开发玻璃墙砖的时候，他说自己想让这些墙砖不但看着舒服，用起来也简单。
“So many times we find materials that we like, and find that technically we can’t use these materials,” Mr. Schatz said. But even with intricate geometric patterns consisting of jewel glass, shell and 24-karat gold, he said, they made the tiles technically sound enough that another contractor or designer “could pick it up and not find it complicated to work with.”
“我们常常找到一些自己喜欢的材料，但从技术角度考虑，却发现它们无法使用，”沙兹说。但即使是用宝石玻璃、贝壳和24k金拼成的复杂几何图形，他说，他们也能在技术上把这些墙砖做得足够牢固，让其他承包商或设计师“能够在选中它后，不会觉得用起来很复杂”。
The Miraflores collection, inspired by Mr. Schatz’s travels to Mexico, Morocco, Portugal and Spain, was introduced last year in stone and now includes seven new designs in glass, for $125 to $375 a square foot. Information: newravenna.com.
这组名为Miraflores的系列作品，灵感来源于沙兹去墨西哥、摩洛哥、葡萄牙和西班牙的几次旅行。去年，该系列发布的主要是石质墙砖，如今又增加了七款新的玻璃墙砖设计，售价为每平方英尺125美元（约合每平方米人民币8294元）至375美元（约合每平方米人民币24894元）。详情参见：newravenna.com
