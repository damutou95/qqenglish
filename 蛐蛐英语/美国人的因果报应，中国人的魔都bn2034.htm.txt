On the surface Daniel Hsia’s “Shanghai Calling” traces an American’s moral comeuppance. But underneath it is a plug for the emerging China, manifested in its most populous city.
表面上看，夏伟(Daniel Hsia)的《纽约客@上海》(Shanghai Calling)讲述的是一个美国人的道德因果报应。但是从骨子说，它是通过中国人口最稠密的城市来讲述正在崛起的中国。
When Sam (Daniel Henney), an ambitious Chinese-American corporate lawyer, is sent to Shanghai against his wishes to secure a contract with a cellphone manufacturer, he bungles the job and must make amends with the help of a circle including Fang Fang (Zhu Zhu), his office assistant; Awesome Wang (Geng Le), a journalist; Amanda (Eliza Coupe), a relocation specialist and single mom; Brad (Sean Gallagher), a randy American English teacher; and Donald (Bill Paxton), a fried-chicken entrepreneur and operator in the fictitious expatriate neighborhood of Americatown.
雄心勃勃的美国华裔公司律师山姆（Sam，丹尼尔·海尼[Daniel Henney]饰）满心不情愿地被派到上海与一家手机生产商签订合同，结果把这个任务搞砸了，必须在一群人的帮助下弥补这个过失，其中包括他的办公室助理芳芳（朱珠饰），聪明的记者王先生（耿乐饰），迁居专家兼单身妈妈阿曼达（Amenda，伊丽莎·库伯[Eliza Coupe]饰），好色的美籍英语老师布拉德（Brad，肖恩·加拉格尔[Sean Gallagher]饰）以及在虚构的“美国城”经营炸鸡生意的唐纳德（Donald，比尔·帕克斯顿[Bill Paxton]饰）。
Of course the haughty Sam has much to learn — about love, about his employers, but especially about Shanghai, whose karaoke bars, parks, high-rise apartments, factories and Western-style coffee shops are shot with glossy loving care by Mr. Hsia.
傲慢的山姆当然有很多东西要学——关于爱，关于他的雇主们，但主要是关于上海，这里的卡拉OK歌厅、公园、高层公寓楼、工厂以及西式咖啡馆都被夏伟满怀感情地拍摄出来。
Though Mr. Hsia, a television alumnus who also wrote the script, has created a somewhat predictable story infused with stereotypes old and new, he gains mileage from light humor, buoyant energy and some appealing performers. Mr. Henney has a handsome, unforced charisma, and Ms. Zhu and Mr. Geng are especially intriguing. And Shanghai itself — gray, sprawling, bursting with energy — exerts the greatest presence of all.
夏伟以前是电视编剧，他也是这部电影的编剧。虽然他写的这个故事多少有些老套，里面充满了各种陈旧的和新出现的成见，但是其中轻松的幽默、上扬的活力以及迷人的演员，给这部戏增加了分量。海尼先生长相英俊，散发着自然的魅力，朱女士和耿先生特别迷人。而戏份最多的还是上海——灰色，庞大，充满活力。
