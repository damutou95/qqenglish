From toothbrushes and towels to pens and pillow cases, you’d be hard-pressed not to find an item with Hello Kitty’s famous mug on it.
从牙刷、毛巾、钢笔到枕套，你很难找到什么东西上面没有Hello Kitty那张著名的脸。
The proof, in fact, is in the dumpling.
实际上，证据在饺子上。
Hong Kong’s first Kitty-dedicated Chinese restaurant opened this month, and those wee whiskers and bow appear atop dumplings (squid ink is used for the eyes, natural food coloring for the bow) and noodles (shredded egg forms the bow in this dish).
本月，香港的第一家Hello Kitty主题中餐厅开业，那些小小的胡须和蝴蝶结出现在饺子（眼睛是用墨鱼汁做成的，蝴蝶结是用天然食用色素做成的）和面条上（蝴蝶结是用鸡蛋碎做成的）。
And late last year a Hello Kitty teahouse made its debut in Kyoto with a similarly styled dim sim menu and matcha green tea lattes. A winter special: hot chocolate with Kitty’s boyfriend Daniel fashioned out of marshmallows and foam.
去年年底，一家Hello Kitty主题茶馆在京都开张，它有风格类似的点心菜单和抹茶拿铁。冬季特饮是热巧克力，上面漂着用棉花糖和泡沫做成的Kitty的男朋友Daniel。
The 40-seat cafe is officially called the Hello Kitty Tea Shop and is open every day from 10:30 a.m. until 5:30 p.m. It is located between two of Kyoto’s most popular sightseeing spots, the Kiyomizu-dera Buddhist temple and the Yasaka Shrine.
这家咖啡馆的官方名字是Hello Kitty Tea Shop，有40个座位，每天上午10点半至下午5点半营业，位于京都最热门的两个景点清水寺和八坂神社之间。
Inside the cafe, Hello Kitty is everywhere, from the porcelain bowls and napkins to the tables and white booths, which are adorned with a pattern of the signature bow. (The Japanese company Sanrio created the character in 1974.)
咖啡馆里到处都是Hello Kitty，从瓷碗、纸巾到桌子和白色隔间，隔间上装饰着标志性的蝴蝶结图案（Hello Kitty是日本的三丽鸥公司1974年设计的）。
Additional menu items include the Namafu parfait with vanilla ice cream, whipped cream and a cookie with Kitty on it, and sweet green tea soup and chestnut soup, both of which come with floating rice cakes made in the likeness of the friendly feline.
菜单上的其他食物包括含有香草冰激凌、生奶油和饼干的面筋冻糕(Namafu parfait)——饼干上有Kitty图案——以及甜绿茶汤和栗子汤，汤上漂浮着这只友好小猫模样的年糕。
